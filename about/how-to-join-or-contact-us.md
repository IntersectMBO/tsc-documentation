# How to Join or Contact Us?

Join the Technical Steering Committee to help guide Cardano's technical evolution through oversight of protocol upgrades, network readiness, and infrastructure security. TSC members provide technical expertise to support safe and sustainable development across the ecosystem. Become a part of the team that drives Cardano’s development on the technical forefront.
